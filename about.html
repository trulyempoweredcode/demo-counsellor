About Me

Hello, I'm Sarah

I'm a BACP-registered counsellor based in the heart of Bath. For several years, I've had the privilege of supporting people through some of the most challenging periods of their lives — and watching them emerge with a clearer sense of who they are and where they want to go.

I trained in integrative counselling, which means I draw on a range of therapeutic approaches — including person-centred therapy, CBT techniques, and psychodynamic ideas — choosing what works best for each individual client. I believe deeply that the relationship between therapist and client is the foundation of all meaningful change.

Outside of the therapy room, I'm a keen walker along the Bath canal towpath, a bookworm, and a slightly obsessive tea drinker. I believe that therapists are human too, and that connection starts with being genuine.

My Approach

How We'll Work Together

Therapy isn't one-size-fits-all. Here's what you can expect when we work together.

1
Step One

A Free Initial Chat

A 15-minute phone call to ask questions, share what's brought you to therapy, and see if we're a good fit. No pressure to commit.

2
Step Two

Your First Session

I'll take time to understand your story and what you'd like to achieve. Together we'll agree on a way of working that feels right for you.

3
Step Three

Ongoing Support

Weekly 50-minute sessions drawing on a range of therapeutic approaches, always adapting to what works best for you.

4
Step Four

Review & Ending

Regular progress reviews. When you're ready, we'll plan a gradual ending so you leave with confidence and tools for wellbeing.

My Values

The Principles That Guide My Work

These values shape every interaction I have with the people I work with.

Compassion First

You're not broken and you don't need fixing. I meet every person with genuine warmth and respect, exactly as they are. My role is to walk alongside you, not to judge.

Safety & Trust

Healing happens when you feel safe. I work hard to create a space where you can be open and vulnerable without fear. Everything shared in our sessions is confidential.

Inclusivity

My practice is open and affirming to people of all backgrounds, identities, and orientations. Everyone deserves access to compassionate mental health support.
