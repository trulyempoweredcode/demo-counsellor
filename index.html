Welcome

Reaching out for support takes real courage

Whether you're feeling overwhelmed by anxious thoughts, weighed down by sadness, or struggling in your relationships, know that these feelings are valid. Taking this step towards support shows real strength.

My practice in Bath offers a warm, non-judgemental space where we can work together at your pace. Using person-centred and integrative approaches, I'll help you make sense of what you're experiencing and find a way forward that feels right for you.

Why Choose Me

A Therapeutic Approach Built on Trust

I believe therapy should feel safe, collaborative, and grounded in genuine human connection.

Qualified & Registered
