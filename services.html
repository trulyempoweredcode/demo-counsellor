Online Sessions

Secure, confidential online sessions via video call for clients who prefer the flexibility of remote appointments or who live outside the local area. Online sessions are just as effective as face-to-face for most people and most conditions.

All you need is a private space, a stable internet connection, and a device with a camera. Sessions are conducted via an encrypted, GDPR-compliant video platform to ensure your privacy is fully protected.

  • Same quality of care as in-person sessions
  • Flexible scheduling including evenings
  • No travel time or parking worries
  • Ideal for busy professionals or parents
  • Available across the UK
