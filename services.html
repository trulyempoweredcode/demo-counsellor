Services & Pricing

Explore the range of counselling support I offer. Every treatment plan is personalised to your unique situation and goals.

Anxiety & Stress Counselling

Anxiety can show up in many ways — from constant worrying and racing thoughts to physical symptoms like a tight chest, difficulty sleeping, or panic attacks. You might find yourself avoiding situations, over-planning, or struggling to switch off.

Together we'll explore what's driving your anxiety and develop practical, sustainable strategies to help you feel calmer and more in control. Many people find that within a few sessions they begin to notice a real difference.

  • Generalised anxiety disorder (GAD)
  • Panic attacks and panic disorder
  • Social anxiety
  • Health anxiety
  • Work-related stress and burnout
  • Phobias

Depression & Low Mood

Depression is more than feeling sad. It can drain your energy, steal your motivation, and make even simple tasks feel overwhelming. You might feel disconnected from the people and activities you used to enjoy, or experience persistent feelings of worthlessness.

We'll work together to understand the patterns that keep depression going and gently begin to shift them. I use a combination of person-centred therapy, behavioural activation, and compassion-focused techniques to help you reconnect with what matters.

  • Persistent low mood
  • Loss of interest or pleasure
  • Fatigue and low motivation
  • Negative self-talk and self-criticism
  • Grief and bereavement
  • Postnatal depression

Relationship Difficulties

Relationships are central to our wellbeing, but they can also be a source of real pain. Whether you're experiencing communication breakdowns, trust issues, recurring arguments, or simply feeling disconnected, counselling can help you make sense of these patterns.

I work with individuals exploring their relationship dynamics — helping you understand your needs, communicate them more effectively, and build healthier connections with the people around you.

  • Communication difficulties
  • Trust and boundary issues
  • Patterns of unhealthy relationships
  • Emotional disconnection
  • Family relationship difficulties
  • Separation and divorce support

Grief & Bereavement

Losing someone close to you — whether through death, the end of a relationship, or another significant life change — can shake the very foundation of your world. Grief doesn't follow a tidy timeline, and there's no right or wrong way to grieve.

I offer a gentle, patient space where you can process your feelings at your own pace, without pressure or expectation. Together we'll honour what you've lost while finding ways to carry on with meaning and connection.

  • Death of a loved one
  • Complicated or prolonged grief
  • Anticipatory grief
  • Loss of a relationship or role
  • Miscarriage and pregnancy loss
  • Supporting children through bereavement

Online Counselling Sessions

I offer secure, confidential online sessions via video call for people who prefer the flexibility of remote counselling or who live outside Bath. Online therapy is just as effective as face-to-face sessions for most concerns.

All you need is a private space, a stable internet connection, and a device with a camera. I use an encrypted, GDPR-compliant video platform to ensure your privacy is fully protected.

  • Same quality of care as in-person sessions
  • Flexible scheduling including evenings
  • No travel time or parking worries
  • Ideal for busy professionals or parents
  • Available across the UK
